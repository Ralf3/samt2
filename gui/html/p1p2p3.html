4. Inputboxes P1, P2 and P3

The three inputboxes P1,P2,P3 are used to assign parameters to SAMT2 or to get short information from SAMT2.
For example fills the Analysis: Info  P1:nrows, P2:ncols, P3:nodata .

The status line shows a short help text and explains the parameters.

Many methods of SAMT2 uses default parameters.
Please be careful using this parameters. They are often a good start, but needs to be adapted in real problems.
